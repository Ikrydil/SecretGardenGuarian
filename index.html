The story, The Secret Garden Guardian, follows a group of tiny heroes led by Super Teacher and including characters like Sir Eggplant, The Red Onion Ronin, General Ant-ares, and Bot-Bot, who protect their secret garden. When the Giant Aphid Army invades, threatening the tomato plants, the Guardians' strength is not enough. Super Teacher guides them to use a clever plan from her magical "Art Book," instructing Bot-Bot to turn on a giant sprinkler. The water washes the Aphid Army away, saving the garden and allowing the heroic team to celebrate their victory.
